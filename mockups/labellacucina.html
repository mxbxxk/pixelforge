A Taste of Italy,
In the Heart of London

Founded by the Moretti family in 1998, La Bella Cucina has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over 25 years. From handmade pasta to wood-fired dishes, every recipe is a tribute to the flavours of northern Italy.
