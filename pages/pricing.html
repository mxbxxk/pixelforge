Pricing FAQs

Are there any monthly fees?▾
No monthly platform fees from us — ever. The only ongoing costs you'd pay are your own domain (typically £10–15/yr) and hosting (typically £5–15/mo depending on your plan). We're transparent about these upfront.
What's included in the quoted price?▾
Design, development, testing, SEO setup, mobile optimisation, and delivery of the finished website. We tell you exactly what's in scope before we start. No surprise invoices.
Can I upgrade my package later?▾
Yes — you can always add pages, features, or functionality later. We'll quote the difference from your original package. Many clients start on Business and upgrade to Professional as they grow.
Do you offer payment plans?▾
For projects over £699, I offer a 50/50 split — 50% deposit to start, 50% on completion. For Enterprise projects I can discuss staged payments. Just mention it when you get in touch.
What does the 20% outreach discount mean?▾
When I reach out to you directly, I can offer a reduced rate because I save on traditional marketing costs. If I contacted you first, just mention the outreach code when you enquire to claim your 20% discount.
