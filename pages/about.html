The Story

Why I Started PixelForge

I started PixelForge Digital after noticing the same thing over and over: great local businesses — tradespeople, restaurants, salons — were either operating without a website or stuck with something embarrassingly outdated that didn't represent how good they actually are.

The problem wasn't that these businesses didn't care — it was that proper agencies charge thousands more than necessary, and DIY builders like Wix or Squarespace produce generic results that don't stand out. I knew I could do better, at a price that actually works for a small business.

Every project I take on is fully bespoke. No page builders, no templates, no cookie-cutter designs. I take the time to understand your business and build something that genuinely represents it — because your website is often the first impression a customer gets.
